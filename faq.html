EduNova AI Help Center

Common Questions

1. What is EduNova AI?

EduNova AI is an AI-powered learning platform that provides notes, quizzes, AI chat, certificates, and progress tracking for students.


2. Is EduNova AI free?

Yes. The basic version is free to use.


3. How do I download notes?

Go to the Notes page, choose a subject, and click the Download Notes button.


4. How do I get a certificate?

Complete the mock test with a good score, then open the Certificate page to view and print it.


5. Does AI Chat require the internet?

The real Gemini AI version requires an internet connection and a valid Google Gemini API key.


6. Can I use EduNova AI on my phone?

Yes. The project is responsive and works on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.


7. Is my progress saved?

Yes. Your progress is stored locally, or in Firebase when cloud storage is enabled.
